My top 3 movies of all time

After

The story essentially follows a young, innocent girl who enters college and unexpectedly falls for a charming young man.
However, he ultimately leads her into a world of peer pressure, shattering her heart and causing her to shy away from love in the future.

The War Room

The War Room features the renowned preacher Priscilla Shirer, who navigates a challenging situation as she learns to trust God for the salvation of her marriage.
Ultimately, she chooses to surrender her struggles to God, marking the beginning of her transformative breakthrough."

The Last Of Us.

This may not be a traditional thriller, but it features a captivating zombie storyline that's a lot of fun.
